    playlistsByGenre becomes Music genre finder
    I am about to have a presentation in front of a whole lecture class. I had to find an interesting subject, and I came up with an idea. How to find the genre of a music without using artificial intelligence? My project is the answer of this question. I need to improve the project for it to be ready on the 10th of October (the day of my presentation).
    I have to make the contribution easier because the project depends on a dictionary binding genres to youtube channels. This way, people will find easier to add more and more data. Also, I thought about a website where people can find the music genre of some songs, and where they can put their contribution with an user-friendly interface.
    The slideshow used will be released shortly after, and will serve as an introduction to the project.
