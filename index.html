My first placement. Automated data analysis (AI).

Detection of logical models on energy data in order to optimize energy consumption. Each survey analyzed had at least 30,000 records (the biggest one had 3M records). To give you an idea, analyzing 30,000 records takes about 10 seconds, and around 5 minutes with 3M records.

Developing context:
- Developed from scratch by me,
- I was managed by a team of three engineers in various energy domains.

Technologies used:
- Python (programming language),
- pandas (data analysis),
- matplotlib (graphics),
- Tkinter (interface),
- SQLite (database),
- EzAPI (progress bar) - my API! ;)
Constraints:
- Respect PEP-8 conventions,
- Respect python conventions,
- Comments,
- Testes,
- Documentation (for the next developers, and the user),
- No object oriented programming,
- The code has to be maintainable by non-professional people.

All the initial objectives were reached, and I could do more than what was expected. The software permits to the engineers to gain two days of works for every survey. Now they only need ONE day of work instead of three days before.
